# 📖 Overview

**NikatAI** is an AI-powered hyperlocal community platform that helps people discover, connect, and transact within trusted neighbourhoods.

Our mission is to create a secure, intelligent, and community-first ecosystem where residents, Resident Welfare Associations (RWAs), local businesses, creators, service providers, and brands can build meaningful local connections backed by trust and Artificial Intelligence.

Rather than relying on fragmented messaging groups or unverified recommendations, NikatAI provides a unified platform for neighbourhood discovery, engagement, and collaboration.

---

# 🎯 Vision

To become India's most trusted AI-powered neighbourhood network by enabling safer local interactions, stronger communities, and intelligent neighbourhood discovery.

---

# ❗ The Problem

Neighbourhood communities often struggle with:

- Unverified recommendations
- Fragmented WhatsApp groups
- Fake local profiles
- Low trust in service providers
- Difficult local discovery
- Poor communication between residents and RWAs
- Limited visibility for local businesses
- Lack of community engagement
- Challenges when relocating to a new locality

NikatAI addresses these challenges by combining **Artificial Intelligence**, **verification**, and **community-driven trust** into one unified platform.
